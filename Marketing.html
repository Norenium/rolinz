Clearly, every successful project has been backed by a strong community.

I believe the blockitop has the potential. Because:

A- We have the critical infrastructure for a successful project.

1- Metaverse

We have the technical knowledge to build a metaverse. More than 80% of the back-end and the blockchain structure is already done. The rest will be done in the process of building.

2- Game-FI

Game development is quite a work.

Structural:

The game-fi ecosystem is mostly based on the 1155 standard, which is ready now. also as it is necessary to be able to improve the game mechanism, the upgradable architecture has been adopted for smart contract development.

Marketing

Before we roll the ball, I want to get on the same page about some definitions:

1- By Marketer, I mean everybody interested in working on "Blockitop" in the field of marketing.
2- Community member: everyone who joined us on any platform.
3- Participants: everybody who bought project assets (in any form with any goal)

At this point, I'm not sure what will motivate marketers to start, but I think a referral program would be interesting.

Everybody can register their wallet address on the smart contract as a marketer and get a referral code.

Marketers can invite people to the platform using a link that contains the code.

Every time someone mints an asset on the project's smart contract (using a code), 20% of the fee goes straight to the Marketer's address.

Based on marketers' suggestions, we may add a discount or some other kind of offer for the participants may be added later.


The community is a counsel where all next steps would be determined. This plan may have some flaws, but they can't be recognized and fixed until we start and go further.
