What is Blockitop

'Blockitop' is a blockchain-based project which allows the creation and trading of virtual goods on the BSC network. It is a platform that allows users to create, trade, and manage digital assets. It is based on the BEP-721 smart contract standard and the main focus is to create a De-Fi ecosystem for managing digital assets. NFTs are digital assets that are similar to cryptocurrencies or digital currencies.

The main feature of NFTs is that they can be used as monetary systems and have different applications in finance, games, art, and commerce. NFTs are becoming more and more popular because they provide various benefits over traditional methods of making and using virtual currencies. For instance, NFTs are more secure, can't be copied, are accessible worldwide, and are transferable without restrictions.




Why NFT

NFTs are created through the BEP-721 protocol which allows developers to issue custom tokens to be traded on the platform. Thus, businesses have many profit-making opportunities by giving their own NFTs. For example, game developers can issue game assets such as characters or items in their games to be traded. This way, they can generate revenue from their creations and promote them worldwide. On the other hand, businesses can also use NFTs for fundraising by issuing them to investors.

NFTs offer several advantages over traditional methods of making and using virtual currencies. One of these advantages is that holding NFTs gives you access to its utilities instead of purchasing them directly from developers. For instance, if you have 'blockitop' NFTs, you can use them for staking or in the game environment. Furthermore, selling your NFTs gives you money for them instantly and lets you make money off of your minted NFT. The process of selling blockitop gives you money instantly while giving the seller access to your utility applications.
